Advanced Frontend Manager - A must have extension that lets your vendors add, import or even export any product(s) from their frontend vendor dashboard.

== Description ==
<strong>Advanced Frontend Manager</strong> is an all in one tool, that lets your vendors <strong>upload</strong> Variable, Grouped, External, Affiliate, Booking, Accommodation, Bundles, Rental, Auction, Simple Subscription and Variable Subscription product types and can easily <strong>import/ export</strong> products – all of this from their <strong>frontend vendor dashboard</strong>, denying(optional) them backend access, thereby not at all compromising on admin’s security.☺
